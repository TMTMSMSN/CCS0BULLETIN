Mission

The College of Computer Studies is dedicated to providing quality education through relevant curriculum, competent faculty, and industry-aligned training that empower students to become effective contributors to the ever-evolving field of computing and information technology.

Objectives

  • To develop technically proficient IT graduates who are capable of designing, developing, and managing information systems.
  • To promote ethical, social, and environmental responsibility in the practice of information technology.
  • To engage in research, innovation, and extension programs that contribute to community and national development.
  • To foster strong linkages with industry partners and continually align academic programs with current technological trends.

Courses Offered (BSIT)

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)
The BSIT program prepares students for a career in the field of Information Technology. It covers the study of computer systems, software development, networking, database management, and systems analysis. Graduates are equipped to design, implement, and manage IT solutions in various business and organizational settings.
